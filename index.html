Account Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your SurgiCount AI account and all associated data, please follow the steps below. This process ensures your privacy rights are respected and your data is permanently removed from our systems.

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

  1. Open the SurgiCount AI app on your device
  2. Log in to your account
  3. Navigate to Settings from the main menu
  4. Scroll down to the "Danger Zone" section
  5. Tap "Delete Account"
  6. Follow the confirmation prompts to permanently delete your account

Option 2: Email Request

  1. Send an email from your registered email address
  2. Include your account email and reason for deletion
  3. We will process your request within 7 business days
  4. You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete
